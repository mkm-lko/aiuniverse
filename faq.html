Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI tools, how to choose the right one, and how AI Universe works

AI Universe is a comprehensive, frontend-only directory of the world's most powerful artificial intelligence platforms. It allows users to discover, compare, and evaluate AI tools across categories like Chatbots, Image Generation, Video, Audio, Coding, Research, Design, Productivity, and Education — all without requiring any sign-up or backend.

Start by identifying your primary use case. If you need general-purpose conversation and writing, try ChatGPT or Claude. For coding assistance, Cursor or GitHub Copilot are top picks. For stunning visuals, go with Midjourney or Leonardo AI. Use the Compare page to see a full feature matrix and narrow down your options, or use the Explore page to filter by category.

Not all tools offer free plans. The Compare page includes a "Free Plan" column that shows which platforms offer a free tier. Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, ElevenLabs, Leonardo AI, Runway, and Cursor offer free access with limitations. Tools like Midjourney, DALL·E, Synthesia, GitHub Copilot, and Notion AI typically require a paid subscription for full access.

Ratings are composite scores (out of 5.0) based on multiple factors: community reviews from major platforms, benchmark performance data, real-world capability assessments, ease of use, reliability, and feature breadth. The ratings aim to reflect both raw capability and practical usability for everyday creators, developers, and researchers.
